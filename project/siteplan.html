Mungunzaya's Project Site Plan

Site Name: E & E Journal Pals

Domain name: journal-pals.com

I chose this name because the website would provide a friendly space for people to record their thoughts and feelings. And 'E & E' is a brand name for my dream company. So it's a friendly journaling website being run by my future 'E & E' brand.

Site Purpose:

The site would provide different activities to engage the user in keeping records of their lives, such as a space for journal entries, habit trackers, list makers, etc.

Scenarios:

- What are the benefits of journaling?

- How can I make journaling fun?
